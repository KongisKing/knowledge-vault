# WealthOS — AI-Powered Wealth Management Platform

## Status
Research Phase

## Summary
Exploring monetizable product ideas built on India's free mutual fund API (mfapi.in). The leading candidate is **WealthOS for CAs** — a B2B SaaS platform targeting India's 800,000 Chartered Accountants, enabling them to give tax-optimized investment guidance to their clients. Scored highest (25/30) among three shortlisted ideas.

## Shortlisted Ideas (Scored by Kong)

| # | Idea | Model | Pricing | Score |
|---|------|-------|---------|-------|
| 1 | **WealthOS for CAs** 🏆 | B2B SaaS | ₹2,000–5,000/month per CA firm | 25/30 |
| 2 | FolioSense | B2B SaaS for SEBI RIAs/MFDs | ₹8,000–25,000/month | 22/30 |
| 3 | RetireRight | AI retirement decumulation advisor | TBD | 21/30 |

## API Foundation

- **mfapi.in** — Free, no-auth Indian mutual fund API
  - 37,462 total schemes
  - 8,232 active schemes
  - No rate limits documented

## Key Decisions

- WealthOS for CAs is the leading idea based on scoring
- B2B model preferred over B2C (larger TAM per customer, stickier)
- India's 800K CAs represent an underserved distribution channel for investment guidance
