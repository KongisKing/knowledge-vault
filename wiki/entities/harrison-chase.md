## Description
Co-founder and CEO of LangChain. One of the most influential figures in the LLM application and agent framework space. Known for democratising LLM app development via open-source tooling.

## Role / Relevance
Author of "Your harness, your memory" — the argument that memory ownership is the central strategic question in agentic AI, and that open harnesses are the only safe path.
